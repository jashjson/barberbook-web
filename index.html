The Problem

Barber Shops Are
Stuck in 1995.

5 lakh+ shops across India. Less than 3% use any digital booking. Customers wait. Barbers lose revenue. Nobody wins.

Uncertain Wait Times
Walk in, sit down, and wonder. Zero visibility into queue position or estimated wait time.
Manual Booking Systems
Phone calls and WhatsApp messages. Slots get forgotten, double-booked, or simply ignored.
Revenue Loss
No-shows and last-minute cancellations create gaps that can't be filled, directly impacting daily earnings.
No Business Intelligence
No data on peak hours, customer patterns, or revenue trends. Decisions made on gut feeling alone.

Features

Everything a Barber Shop
Actually Needs.

Token Queue System
FCFS token assignment. Book online, get your number, know your position before leaving home.
Real-time Updates
WebSocket-powered live queue. Every phone updates instantly when status changes — no refresh.
Choose Your Barber
Independent queue per barber. Pick who you want, see their availability, book directly.
Easy Reschedule
Cancel or reschedule with one tap. Your freed slot becomes available for others instantly.
Owner Analytics
Daily revenue, peak hours, barber performance — all in a clean dashboard built for shop owners.
Push Notifications
Customers get notified when their turn is close. Fewer no-shows. Less idle time. More revenue.
